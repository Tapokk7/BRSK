// ABOUT THE WORK

A Legend Born of Darkness

Berserk is a dark fantasy series by Kentaro Miura, serialized in Young Animal magazine since 1989. The story follows Guts — the Black Swordsman, whose life is defined by rage, pain, and an unyielding will to survive.

The world of Berserk is a medieval hell where demons exist alongside humans, fate is etched into flesh by the Brand of Sacrifice, and the only response to powerlessness is a massive sword and unbridled fury.

Miura passed away in May 2021, leaving the manga unfinished. His students continue the work based on his notes.
